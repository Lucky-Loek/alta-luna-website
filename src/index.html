About us

We're a new indierock band from Zeeland, The Netherlands. Inspired by the great bands of our youth - Arctic Monkeys, Blur, The Strokes - we're exploring sounds that we would love to let you hear. Fat drums, riffy guitars and big choruses mean it's always new, always unique, always recognizable Alta Luna. On 25 June our single The Chain will hit your favorite streaming platform. So give us a listen and maybe tell your friends..?
